# easyGDS Website Sitemap

**Key:**
* **[P] Page:** A unique URL (e.g., easygds.com/airlines).
* **[C] Content:** A section or module located within that page (no unique URL).

---

## 1. Primary Navigation

### **[P] Home**
* **[C] Hero Section:** Unified value proposition for the travel commerce ecosystem.
* **[C] Segment Selector:** Direct entry points for Airlines, Travel Agents, and Loyalty & Retail.
* **[C] The "Engine" Preview:** High-level overview of underlying technology and inventory scale.
* **[C] Social Proof:** Client logo bar featuring Etihad, Malaysia Airlines, and Amadeus.

## 2. Solutions (Segment Positioning)

### **[P] For Airlines**
* **[C] Value Proposition:** Focus on ancillary revenue, PSS integration, and holiday brand building.
* **[C] Holiday Platform:** Module for dynamic packaging and fixed tour management.
* **[C] Stopover Experiences:** Tools for transit tourism and PNR-based upsells.
* **[C] B2B Distribution:** Extending airline content to 3rd party agencies.
* **[C] Pricing:** Commercial subsection detailing revenue share and enterprise models.

### **[P] For Travel Agents**
* **[C] Value Proposition:** Focus on global inventory access and markup control.
* **[C] B2B/B2C Portals:** White-label storefronts and multi-hierarchy agency management.
* **[C] Booking Tools:** Flight + Hotel bundling and automated workflow features.
* **[C] Pricing:** Commercial subsection detailing SaaS subscription tiers or transaction fees.

### **[P] For Loyalty & Retail**
* **[C] Value Proposition:** Focus on customer engagement and "Earn & Burn" mechanics.
* **[C] Redemption Platform:** Dynamic payment options (Cash + Points) for travel rewards.
* **[C] Vertical Launch:** Speed-to-market tools for non-travel retailers to enter the industry.
* **[C] Pricing:** Commercial subsection detailing partnership models and integration costs.
